Welcome!

This is my website for my class project

Welcome! This website is going to explore a couple of songs from the musician Phil Elverum in hopes to attract old and new fans of his work. We will dive into his third studio album “The Glow Pt.2” under the name The Microphones. I will talk about what I believe some of the lyrics means, the themes found in each song, and how the music evokes these topics. You will be able to learn more about me, the artist, the songs, and you can contact me as well via email in the following webpages. Thank you for coming, I hope you enjoy learning about Phil Elverum’s work and become a fan.
